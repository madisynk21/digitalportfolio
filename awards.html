Awards

2023 Congressional App Challenge Winner

My app CarbonCalc won the 2023 Congressional App Challenge. CarbonCalc is a carbon footprint calculator I developed inspired by an assignment in my environmental science class where I had to calculate my carbon footprint by hand. Originally developed in JavaScript on Code.org, CarbonCalc is comprised of two features: a quiz game to teach users about carbon emmissions and calculator where users can calucalte their personal emmisions in different activities. Since winning I have had the oppurtunity to display my app at the US Capitol building at House of Code 2024. I have also recreated CarbonCalc in Swift and SwiftUI and am in the process of putting it on the app store.
